We are introducing our new sim steering wheel

Authentic Design

We put the utmost effort into recreating the real steering wheel by using the CAD data provided by MGP. Applying many real-world manufacturing processes, the wheel represents the pinnacle of motorsport bringing Formula One level of intricacy right into your own hands.

Extremely Lightweight

The main body consists of a handcrafted carbon fiber shell, which provides unmatched rigidity. The Force Feedback is transferred with precision from the wheelbase to your hands. The total weight of the wheel is 1240 grams.

Advanced Controls

Every button, rotary, and function has been designed for optimal performance and accessibility. Making rapid adjustments without losing focus and maintaining peak performance lap after lap. This is where the real-world requirements transfer perfectly to the sim racing world.
